Hack Noel 2024

Completed

Hack Noel is an end-of-year hackathon hosted by Youth Code Camp. It brings together students from high schools across different communities. Over 60 students participated in Hack Noel 2024, working on projects like web apps, mobile apps, chatbots, and innovative solutions involving IoT and AI to address challenges in healthcare, education, and community services.
